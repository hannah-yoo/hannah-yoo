## Hi there 👋  I'm Hannah
I am a freelance designer and visual artist straddling the line between tangible tactile experiences and digital environments. Having trained in both **Design and Fine Arts**, my practice centers around translating the raw, physical materiality of analog mediums into the fluid, interactive landscape of the web.

## 🚀 Expertise
- Material Culture & Tech Integration: Researching and designing at the intersection of tangible artifacts and technology like responsive interfaces.
- Brand Narratives & Identity: Translating fine art concepts into structural brand identities and cohesive digital environments.
- Analog to Digital Mediums: Reimagining traditional tactile mediums within the modern web ecosystem to create distinct, high-end user experiences.
